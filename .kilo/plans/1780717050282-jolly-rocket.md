# Plan: Create potential_alt_alphas.md

## Task
Map 10-20 logically relevant econ factors from `econ_get_factors.csv` (~4000 factors) to each of 23 futures, producing `potential_alt_alphas.md`.

## Available Factor Categories (key ones)
1. **CPI** - food sub-indices (grain, oil, eggs, meat, sugar, vegetables, fruit), clothing, housing, transport, medical
2. **PPI industry-specific** - coal/coke, petroleum, ferrous/non-ferrous metallurgy, chemical, rubber/plastics, textile, food processing, building materials, electricity
3. **PPIRM** - raw material purchase prices by category: fuel/power, ferrous metals, non-ferrous metals, chemical, agricultural, textile, building materials, timber/pulp
4. **PMI** - manufacturing PMI (production, new orders, new export orders, raw material inventory, purchase price, finished goods inventory, import), non-manufacturing PMI (services, construction), sub-indices by enterprise size
5. **Macro** - GDP growth, CPI-PPI spread, social financing scale (aggregate credit), M2 (if available), loan rates
6. **CPI-PPI差值** - inflation剪刀差

## Factor Selection Criteria
- Prefer YoY (同比) over MoM (环比) for signal stability
- Prefer national-level over regional data
- Prefer headline indices + most relevant sub-indices
- Include both supply-side (PPI/PPIRM/PMI) and demand-side (CPI/credit) signals
- Include macro backdrop factors (GDP, social financing) where relevant
